About The Business


This business's main objective is to restore worn, damaged, and old clothes back to it's almost original state and then get them back into the community and used once again.


We usually obtained our inventory through thrift shops, garage sales, and also donations and then we restore them here at our location.


Also, we do accept donations all hors of the week, so if want to donate items contact us via our contact page or visit our location.
